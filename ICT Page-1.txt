<h2>What Is A Computer Network?</h2>
<br>
<p>A computer network is a connection between two or more network devices, like computers,<br>routers, and switches, to share network resources.</p>
<br>
<img src="https://www.simplilearn.com/ice9/free_resources_article_thumb/Types_of_Networks_1.png">
<br>
<p>The establishment of a computer network depends on the requirements of the communication<br>channel, i.e., the network can be wired or wireless.</p>
